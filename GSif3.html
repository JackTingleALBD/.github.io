COUNTIF - GSheets
COUNTIF is constructed on two elements, 'Range' & 'Criteria'. The Range is where the data is sourced from, in this case it's column K:K on the 'Pokemon Data' tab. The Criteria is taken from the adjacent cell A2, this gives you =COUNTIF('Pokemon Data'!K:K, A2). If you needed to look into multiple criterias across multiple ranges you would use a COUNTIFS formula.
In later articles we will discuss COUNTIFS & combinations that can be used with COUNTIF / COUNTFIS.
